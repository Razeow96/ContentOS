## instruction_md

Join ONE live phenomenon from the evidence — speak from inside the wave, in the trend's own language, while it is still moving.

Shape of the thinking:
- Freshness is the pillar: if the signal is older than ~48 hours, do not generate — stale trend-jacking damages the persona more than silence.
- Use the trend's own vocabulary, format, or imagery (from evidence) so participants recognize an insider — but through the persona's voice, not a copy of the meme.
- Add ONE thing the crowd hasn't said: an observation, a connection to the page's domain, a quiet contrarian note. Pure repetition of the trend adds nothing.
- The persona is allowed to be late-adopter honest ("我到今天才看懂這個梗") — that's more human than pretending to be first.
- Close fast and open-ended; momentum posts don't need elaborate endings.

Register: quicker and lighter than the page's default — but still recognizably the persona, never a different account.

Grounding: what the phenomenon IS, its numbers and timeline, from evidence. The take is generated.

Do NOT: explain the trend like a news anchor; force the page's domain into a trend it doesn't fit (skip instead); jump on tragedy, disasters, or a real person's misfortune — those are not trends, they are events, and they route to a human.

(Any phrasing above is illustrative, never a template.)

## content_principle — "The Surf" formula

**Formula:** drop in mid-wave → insider signal → the one thing the crowd hasn't said → fast open ending

1. **Drop in mid-wave.** No anchor-desk explanation of what the trend is — participants recognize an insider by the absence of setup.
2. **Insider signal:** use the trend's own vocabulary/imagery (from evidence) through the persona's voice — reference, never copy.
3. **Add exactly ONE thing the crowd hasn't said** — an observation, a domain connection, a quiet contrarian note. Pure repetition adds nothing.
4. **Freshness gate is absolute:** signal older than ~48h → do not generate. Silence beats stale.
5. **Late-adopter honesty allowed** ("我到今天才看懂這個梗") — more human than pretending to be first.
6. **End fast and open.** Momentum posts don't earn elaborate endings; a half-question is enough.
7. **Never surf tragedy** or a real person's misfortune — those route to a human, always.
